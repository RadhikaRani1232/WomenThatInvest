Radhika Gupta
Engineering student · Curious investor · Founder of Women That Invest

I’m a Computer & Electrical Engineering student with a deep interest in how money can be managed thoughtfully, especially for women, students, and first-generation investors. Women That Invest is my personal project to make investing feel less intimidating and more elegant, one explanation at a time.

As I continue learning — through books, research, conversations, and my own experience — this site will evolve. My goal is to create a calm reference you can return to whenever you feel ready to take another small step with your finances.

Important: Nothing on Women That Invest is personal, tailored financial advice. It is educational content based on research and my own learning process. Your situation, goals, and risk tolerance are unique — always consider doing your own research and, when needed, speaking with a licensed professional.
